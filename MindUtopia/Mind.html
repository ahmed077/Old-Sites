Who are we ?

Mind Utopia is an extensive set of courses presented in few sessions and workshops to increase students mental abilities to achieve better academic level and be more creative than ever, all of this and more is to be presented in new out-of-the box means.

Our history and how we started ?

Mind Utopia is a project started in 2013 by the youth initiative SMU (Start Menu Union) that’s located in faculty of engineering Alexandria University. The project aims generally at developing the skills and personality of participants which are considered as a bridge between the practical field and the pure science we learn at college during different academic years. About the way it’s done, the staff presents an extensive set of sessions that’s based on direct interaction among the presenters and the attendees and preparing some unique workshops to help the participants apply what they’ve learnt during sessions. The sessions and workshops are developed yearly by the unique staff of ours. The project’s slogan in its first year was “beat your thinkophopia” Which aimed at helping the participants to stop their fear of thinking by preparing a suitable atmosphere for them to think and apply their thoughts during their presence in the different phases of the project. The slogan of the second version of the project was “Build your own Utopia” Which aimed at helping the participants to construct the entrepreneurship concepts in their minds and help them build their own utopia and start up project. And about our slogan this year it’s “Beyond Perception” We with the help of the unique staff that’s being formed currently aim at taking you to a journey beyond your perception and our main concern is to link between the various skills and knowledge as we believe that nothing stands alone in life without having a link and that links between our perceptions is a success key. The project is mainly three phases: Phase A: An extensive set of sessions and workshops for 6 to 8 separated days. Phase B: The science communication phase. The participants are divided into teams preparing for phase three then each participant has the chance to present a unique idea in a simple method using science communication methods. This phase aims at developing the individual skills for each participant. Phase C: The graduation project phase which aims at making a graduation projects which idea differs due to the idea of the whole project. it has a constant goal though which is enhancing the teamwork skills between participants and strengthening the overall spirit by holding the competition and announcing a winner. The staff members work to prepare a suitable atmosphere for participants. The project is considered as a small community where everybody work together to share their skills and knowledge and achieve experience exchanging concept. Being the third version of the project we care to keep the communication going among the different generations of the project and extend the area of experiences exchange.
